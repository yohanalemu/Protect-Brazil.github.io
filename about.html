About

Understanding Brazil's challenges, one step toward justice and peace.

Our Values

Welcome to Protect Brazil! We are a group of web development students from Middle Tennessee State University (MTSU) passionate about using technology to address global issues. Through this project, we aim to shed light on the complex social, economic, and systemic factors contributing to crime in Brazil.


Aligned with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal #16, we aim to promote peace, justice, and strong institutions by fostering awareness and transparency. As future web developers, we believe technology can drive meaningful change, and this website is our step toward that vision.

We value transparency and accessibility, offering interactive maps, data visualiaztions, and engaging stories. By exploring the root causes of crime, such as social inequality and economic disparity, we aim to foster understanding and compassion. Together, we can support organizations striving for justice and reform, creating a safer Brazil for everyone.

How We Help

-Educational Resources for Understanding Crime and Its Impact

-Interactive Maps to Explore Crime Data Across Brazil

-Case Studies Highlighting Real-Life Experiences and Solutions

-Safety Tips to Navigate and Protect Yourself in Brazil

-Get Involved with Organizations Promoting Justice and Reform
